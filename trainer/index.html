About Me

Mark is a strength and conditioning coach working out of Gravity Fitness (@gravityfitdub) and Results Fitness (@resultsfitness) in Dublin. Having graduated from DCU with a Bachelor of Science in Sports Science and Health, Mark moved to America (Austin,TX) to gain valuable experince in the fitness industry.

He spent a year working in Golds Gym, acquring the American College of Sports Medicines; Certified Personal Trainer along the way. Having returned to Ireland in December 2016 Mark has been working hard to improve even further and now wants to branch out into the online fitness industry.

Mark has an extensive sporting background having played a wide range of sports from a young age and still plays rugby to this day. Giving him an advantage in having insight into the needs and wants of a sports player as well as the general fitness enthusiast.
